# Just Between Us … and Science: The Women's Health Lab

> A podcast by Dr. Patrícia Mota, PT, PhD

## Overview

This is the official website for "Just Between Us … and Science," a podcast dedicated to women's health, hosted by Dr. Patrícia Mota. The podcast explores the latest research in women's health, from pregnancy and postpartum to hormones and pelvic health, making science accessible and practical.
